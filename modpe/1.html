How to make Modpkg file ModPE

Do you Want to Combined Texture and Mod and Protect your Codes? with Modpkg you can do that. Modpkg is a file which Contains Texture and Mod, Modpkg is the best way to hide your Codes.

Now we will make Modpkg step by step:

  • First make 2 folders and name it with "images" and "script".

  • Place your Textures in Folder "images".

  • And Place your Mod in Folder "script".

  • After Placing the Textures and Mod in the Folder, make the Folders Archieve in 1 zip

  • Then Rename it to "name.modpkg" Change .zip to .Modpkg.

  • Now you can play it, Enjoy !
